# Continuity Is An Operational Property

## Concept

Continuity is not just memory. It is the ability for a workflow to preserve
intent, constraints, decisions, and review state across time.

## Why It Matters

AI-assisted work becomes fragile when each session has to reconstruct the
operational situation from scattered fragments.

## Public Model

A supervised workflow should make the current state legible enough for humans
and assistants to resume work without losing the boundary between guidance and
authority.

## Boundary

The goal is not autonomous intelligence. The goal is governed continuity between
human intent and assisted execution.
